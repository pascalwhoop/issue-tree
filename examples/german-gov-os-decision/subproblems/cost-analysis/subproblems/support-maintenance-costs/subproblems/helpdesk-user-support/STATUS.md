## Summary

Linux provides 15-20% lower steady-state helpdesk costs (€350M-€525M/year) compared to Windows (€420M-€630M/year) for 500K workstations, but requires 2-3 years of elevated transition costs. 5-year net savings: €70M-€210M. 10-year net savings: €200M-€1.3B.
