# Analysis: Organizational Readiness Assessment

## Problem Understanding

The question asks: How organizationally ready is the German government for large-scale OS change, considering cultural factors, change capacity, political environment, structural factors, and historical precedents?

This is an assessment question that requires evaluating readiness across multiple interconnected dimensions for a complex, multi-level governmental organization.

## Why SOLVE (Not Decompose)

While this question has multiple dimensions to assess, it should be solved as a unified analysis because:

1. **Integrated assessment needed**: Readiness dimensions are interconnected (e.g., leadership alignment affects change capacity, cultural factors influence structural effectiveness)
2. **Comparative evaluation required**: Must compare across dimensions and levels to reach overall readiness verdict
3. **Holistic judgment necessary**: Cannot simply aggregate sub-assessments - need integrated professional judgment
4. **Single research methodology**: Same research approach (examining German government context, IT history, organizational frameworks) applies across all dimensions
5. **Artificial decomposition risk**: Breaking into "cultural readiness," "change capacity," etc. as separate nodes would create artificial boundaries and require duplicate research
