# Analysis: Stakeholder Mapping and Concerns

## Understanding of the Problem

The question asks for comprehensive stakeholder mapping across the complex German government structure (federal, state, local) for OS decisions. This requires identifying:
1. Key stakeholder groups at each level
2. Their power/influence and interest levels
3. Specific concerns about OS changes
4. Motivations driving their positions
5. Potential champions and blockers

## Why I Chose to Solve

This problem can be answered directly through targeted research because:
- It's a focused analytical question about stakeholder identification
- The dimensions (concerns, motivations, influence) are aspects of describing stakeholders, not separate questions
- Research methods include: German government structure analysis, IT governance frameworks, case studies
- Stakeholder mapping is a well-established methodology I can apply
