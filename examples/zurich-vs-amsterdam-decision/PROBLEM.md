# Problem: Should I Move to Zurich or Stay in Amsterdam?

## Question

Should I (Head of Engineering at Evercure) relocate from Amsterdam to Zurich, or remain in Amsterdam?

## Context

### Personal Background
- **Current role**: Head of Engineering at Evercure
- **Nationality**: German
- **Partner**: Italian
- **Current location**: Amsterdam (1-3 years established)
- **Property**: Not yet purchased, considering buying
- **Family**: No children yet

### Key Priorities (in order of importance)
1. Lifestyle & culture
2. Family & relationships
3. Cost of living

### Primary Driver
Financial reasons - evaluating tax benefits and salary potential

### Values & Preferences
- Being surrounded by brilliant people doing meaningful work in:
  - Life sciences
  - Medical research
  - Healthcare research
- Strong community of people who enjoy:
  - Sports
  - Outdoors activities
- Intellectual stimulation and professional network quality matter significantly

### Current Situation
- Established in Amsterdam (1-3 years) but not deeply rooted
- No property purchased yet (flexibility to move)
- Partner considerations (Italian national) are important
- Career is in tech/engineering within healthcare sector (Evercure)

## Success Criteria

A good solution will:
- Provide a clear recommendation (Zurich or Amsterdam) with strong reasoning
- Analyze all major factors: financial, lifestyle, career, relationships, quality of life
- Consider both short-term impacts and long-term implications
- Address partner's situation and preferences (Italian national)
- Evaluate property purchase timing and location
- Consider the professional network and community alignment with values
- Account for both current situation and future planning (potential children, career growth)

## Notes

- Zurich considerations: Known for life sciences (Roche, Novartis, ETH), strong outdoors culture, German-speaking, tax structure
- Amsterdam considerations: Current established life, international community, existing network, different tax structure
- This is a significant life decision with multiple interdependent factors
- Financial details (current salary, offers) are available but not documented here for privacy
