# Analysis

## Problem Understanding

Comparing career opportunities and professional network quality in healthcare/life sciences engineering between Zurich and Amsterdam. Focus on ecosystem strength, research institutions, companies, talent density, and alignment with values around meaningful work.

## Research Plan

1. **Healthcare/Life Sciences Companies**: Map major pharma, biotech, medtech companies in each city
2. **Research Institutions**: Identify universities, research centers, hospitals doing cutting-edge work
3. **Startup Ecosystem**: Analyze healthtech/biotech startup density and funding
4. **Talent Pool**: Assess engineering talent concentration and quality
5. **Networking**: Identify professional communities, events, conferences
6. **Career Growth**: Evaluate job market depth and advancement opportunities

## Key Questions
- Which city has stronger life sciences cluster?
- Where are the "brilliant people doing meaningful work"?
- Which offers better long-term career trajectory?
- How does language (German advantage) affect opportunities?
