# Sub-Problem: Career & Professional Network

## Question

How do Zurich and Amsterdam compare in terms of career opportunities and professional community quality for someone working in healthcare/life sciences engineering?

## Context

This addresses a core value: being surrounded by brilliant people doing meaningful work in life sciences, medical research, and healthcare research. The parent problem is deciding whether to relocate from Amsterdam to Zurich.

**Key factors to analyze:**
- Healthcare/life sciences ecosystem strength in each city
- Major companies and research institutions (e.g., Roche, Novartis, ETH Zurich vs Amsterdam options)
- Engineering talent pool and community quality
- Startup and innovation ecosystem in healthcare tech
- Professional networking opportunities and events
- Career growth potential in current role or future roles
- Remote work implications (if relevant for Evercure role)

**Personal context:**
- Currently Head of Engineering at Evercure
- Values intellectual stimulation and being around brilliant people
- Specifically interested in life sciences/medical/healthcare research work
- German national (language advantage in Zurich)

## Acceptance Criteria

A good answer will:
- Map out the healthcare/life sciences ecosystem in both cities
- Identify key companies, research institutions, and innovation hubs
- Assess quality and size of professional communities
- Compare networking opportunities and professional events
- Evaluate career advancement prospects in each location
- Consider how each city aligns with values around meaningful work
- Provide clear conclusion on which location better serves career and professional network goals
