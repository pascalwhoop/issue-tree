# Analysis

## Problem Understanding

Need to compare the comprehensive financial implications of Zurich vs Amsterdam for a Head of Engineering in healthcare, including taxes, cost of living, property, and wealth accumulation.

## Research Plan

1. **Tax Structures**: Research 2026 tax rates for both Switzerland (Zurich canton) and Netherlands
2. **Salary Expectations**: Research typical Head of Engineering salaries in both cities
3. **Cost of Living**: Compare housing, transportation, food, utilities, insurance
4. **Property Market**: Analyze purchase costs and market conditions in both cities
5. **Net Position Calculation**: Calculate take-home pay minus living costs
6. **Long-term Wealth**: Consider savings rate, wealth accumulation over 5-10 years

## Research Sources
- Tax authority data for both countries
- Cost of living databases (Numbeo, Expatistan)
- Property market data
- Salary surveys for tech leadership roles
- Recent 2025-2026 data for accuracy
