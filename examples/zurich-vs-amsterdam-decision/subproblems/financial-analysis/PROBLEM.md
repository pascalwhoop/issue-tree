# Sub-Problem: Financial Analysis

## Question

What are the comprehensive financial implications of moving to Zurich versus staying in Amsterdam for a Head of Engineering at Evercure?

## Context

This sub-problem addresses the primary driver (financial reasons) for considering the move. The parent problem is deciding whether to relocate from Amsterdam to Zurich.

**Key factors to analyze:**
- Tax structures (Switzerland vs Netherlands) for both individuals
- Cost of living comparison (housing, transportation, food, services)
- Salary expectations in each location for Head of Engineering role
- Property purchase implications in each city
- Long-term wealth accumulation potential
- Partner's work situation and income potential (Italian national)

**Personal context:**
- Currently Head of Engineering at Evercure (healthcare sector)
- 1-3 years in Amsterdam
- No property purchased yet
- Partner is Italian national
- No children yet

## Acceptance Criteria

A good answer will:
- Provide concrete tax comparison data for both locations
- Compare real cost of living with specific examples (rent, groceries, transport)
- Analyze property purchase costs and market conditions
- Calculate net financial position difference (take-home pay minus living costs)
- Consider both short-term cash flow and long-term wealth building
- Address financial implications for both partners
- Provide clear conclusion on which location is more financially advantageous
