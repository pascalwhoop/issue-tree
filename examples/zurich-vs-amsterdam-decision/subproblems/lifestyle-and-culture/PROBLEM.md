# Sub-Problem: Lifestyle & Culture

## Question

How do Zurich and Amsterdam compare in terms of lifestyle fit, cultural environment, and alignment with personal values around sports/outdoors and social life?

## Context

This is the TOP PRIORITY in the decision. The parent problem is deciding whether to relocate from Amsterdam to Zurich.

**Key factors to analyze:**
- Sports and outdoors culture (hiking, skiing, cycling, water sports, etc.)
- Quality and accessibility of outdoor activities
- Social life and community vibe (international vs local, pace of life)
- Cultural fit (Swiss culture vs Dutch culture)
- Language environment (German advantage in Zurich, English proficiency)
- City personality and values alignment
- Work-life balance culture
- Weather and geography
- International community and expat experience

**Personal context:**
- German national (language advantage in Zurich, but established in Amsterdam)
- Strong value for sports and outdoors activities
- 1-3 years in Amsterdam (knows the city, has some social connections)
- Lifestyle & culture is stated as top priority

## Acceptance Criteria

A good answer will:
- Compare outdoor and sports opportunities in detail (Alps access vs Dutch cycling/water)
- Assess cultural fit for a German in each city
- Evaluate social life and community characteristics
- Consider work-life balance and quality of life factors
- Analyze how each city's personality aligns with stated values
- Factor in language and integration considerations
- Account for transition cost (leaving established Amsterdam life vs new Zurich opportunities)
- Provide clear conclusion on which location better serves lifestyle and cultural priorities
