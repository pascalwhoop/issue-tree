# Analysis

## Problem Understanding

Need to assess long-term quality of life for future family planning, considering childcare, education, healthcare, safety, stability, retirement, and how life evolves over 10-20+ years.

## Research Plan

1. **Family support & childcare**: Parental leave, childcare costs, availability
2. **Education systems**: Quality from kindergarten through university
3. **Healthcare**: System quality, costs, accessibility
4. **Safety & environment**: Crime, air quality, green spaces
5. **Economic/political stability**: Long-term outlook, stability
6. **Retirement & pensions**: Long-term financial planning
7. **Housing market**: Long-term investment prospects
8. **Family community**: Support systems for young families

## Key Questions
- Which city is better for raising children?
- Where will we be better off in 10, 20, 30 years?
- Which offers more stable, predictable future?
- What are the trade-offs in family life quality?
